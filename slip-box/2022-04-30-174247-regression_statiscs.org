#+title: Regression (statistics)
To regress a variable with respect to other variables means to find some linear combination of those variables that explains the primary variable (all of them as time series).
Something of the form:
$Y_t=\beta_0+X_{t1}\beta_1+...+X_{tk}\beta_k+\varepsilon_t$
