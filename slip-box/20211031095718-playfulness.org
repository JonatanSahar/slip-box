#+title: Playfulness 

Dramatic in its importance in learning - from [[id:20210627T195201.530286][Feldenkrais]] to [[id:20211031T095802.846452][Meditation]] (especially in the dharma taught by [[id:20211031T100247.836351][Rob Burbea]] Nathanm and Zohar ).
It depends on  [[id:20211031T095904.332812][Not being afraid to make mistakes]].
When we play this way we can really be creative, we can try new things, and we can discover new connections (see [[id:20210627T195203.301327][למידה היא גילוי של קשרים]])
