#+title: standard error
In [[id:20210627T195238.608117][Statistics]].
It's the [[id:20220306T135846.426314][standard deviation]] of the *estimate* of the population mean.
I.e, it's the SD of the distribution of *sample means*, where each value is the mean of a sample from the population. (The distribution of sample means in centered around the population )
